About me

I was born in Jieyang, Guangdong, China. I received the B.Sc. degree in Opto-electronics Information Science and Engineering from South China Normal University in 2019, under the supervision of Prof. Xuguang Huang. I am currently doing the M.Sc. degree in Microelectronics and Solid State Electronics at Peking University (Postgraduate Recommendation), under the supervision of Assoc. Prof. Qian Li.
